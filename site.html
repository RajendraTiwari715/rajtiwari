About Śrī Kathāvachak
Bridging ancient scriptures with contemporary life

Pujya Pandit Shri Arvind Krishna Shastri Ji Maharaj is a revered Kathavachak and spiritual guide whose divine voice and profound knowledge illuminate the path of devotion and righteousness. Through his soulful narrations, he has touched countless hearts and inspired a deeper connection with Sanatan Dharma. He is widely known for conducting sacred discourses including Shrimad Bhagwat Katha, Ram Katha, Shiv Katha, and Devi Bhagwat, where his words beautifully blend devotion, wisdom, and life-transforming teachings.

Beyond Kathas, Maharaj Ji is also a highly respected expert in Karmkand, Anusthan, and all types of religious Yagyas, guiding devotees in performing rituals with authenticity and spiritual precision. Having received his traditional and spiritual education from the holy cities of Varanasi and Vrindavan—renowned centers of Vedic learning and devotion—he continues to spread the light of spirituality. Currently residing in Itwa Chitrakoot, Maharaj Ji serves society through his divine teachings, remaining a guiding force for devotees seeking peace, purpose, and devotion in their lives.

🌺 About Maa Sharda Shakti Dham Lok Kalyan Trust
Dedicated to the upliftment of society through spiritual, cultural, and charitable activities

Maa Sharda Shakti Dham Lok Kalyan Trust is a sacred initiative dedicated to the upliftment of society through spiritual, cultural, and charitable activities. Guided by the divine vision of Pujya Pandit Shri Arvind Krishna Shastri Ji Maharaj, the trust works tirelessly to promote the values of Sanatan Dharma and serve humanity with devotion.

The core mission of the trust is to organize and support religious activities, Kathas, Yagyas, Anusthans, and other dharmik events, ensuring that the light of spirituality reaches every corner of society. With a strong commitment to faith and service, the trust strives to take dharmik initiatives to greater heights and inspire future generations.

🐄 Maa Sharda Shakti Dham Gaushala

Under the noble guidance of the trust, the Maa Sharda Shakti Dham Gaushala has been established as a symbol of compassion and seva. Here, cows are nurtured, protected, and served with utmost care and devotion.

Gaushala is not just a place, but a living embodiment of Gau Seva, where every effort is made to ensure the well-being, nourishment, and safety of cows. It reflects the deep-rooted cultural and spiritual significance of cow protection in Sanatan Dharma.

Through this initiative, the trust spreads awareness about the importance of cow service, kindness, and sustainable living, encouraging society to participate in this noble cause.

🌼 Vision & Purpose

  • To promote and preserve Sanatan Dharma and cultural values
  • To organize and expand religious and spiritual activities
  • To serve society through seva, गौ सेवा (cow service), and charitable works
  • To take dharmik initiatives to greater heights and wider reach
  • To inspire people towards a life of faith, compassion, and righteousness
